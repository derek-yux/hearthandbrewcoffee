Our Story

Nestled in the vibrant streets of Toronto, Hearth & Brew was born from a simple dream: to create a space that feels like home, no matter where you're from.


Our café combines the warmth of a living room with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee. Every corner is designed with comfort in mind—from our plush armchairs to the crackling fireplace that warms our space during those long Canadian winters.


We source our beans from ethical farms around the world and roast them in-house to bring you the perfect cup every time. Our baked goods are made fresh daily, using recipes passed down through generations.


Whether you're looking for a quiet corner to work, a cozy spot to read, or a friendly place to meet with friends, Hearth & Brew is your home away from home.
